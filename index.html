What does it mean to be a research university?

Every year the NSF publishes research expenditures across universities, sliced by field.

Displayed are the 50 highest-ranked institutions, viewed across 32 subfields. Click a university name or field to re-sort.

The distribution is the story. Some universities concentrate almost everything in one or two fields and barely register elsewhere. Others show up across every column without leading any of them. A few anchor a family of related disciplines — engineering, or life sciences — and fade outside it. These aren't random patterns. They're the accumulated result of decades of hiring, funding, and institutional identity. Sort the columns. You'll start to see the shapes.
